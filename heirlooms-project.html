Heirloom

Website Development | VCU Handmade Web

A labor of love. Heirloom was my final project for my favorite class, Handmade Web. It is an homage to oral tradition and sisterhood, a cookbook, a family archive, an experimental platform, and my first ever project of personal illustrations.

The assignment was to create a website that felt personal to me - something always under construction, that reflected the nature of the web. I felt compelled to try out jQueryUI's drag-&-drop function and incoorporate handmade illustrations; an interactive game-like archive of the recipes I grew up with felt like the perfect union.
