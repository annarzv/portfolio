fishries

Over 90% of Pakistan's fish & seafood pass through this harbour. began with the small fishing village of Kolachi, founded by fishing communities, and has since evolved into a modern, mechanized industry.

The first modern fish harbor was built in 1958, leading to a significant growth in the fishing fleet and the development of commercial fishing sectors, particularly for shrimp, which earns substantial foreign exchange.
