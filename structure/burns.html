burns road

Officially, Burns Road is named Muhammad Bin Qasim Rd. But I don't know a single person that refers to it as such.

Burns Road is a well known "food street" in Karachi, where many restaurants predate Independence. It was named after a Scottish doctor & spy called James Burnes who was stationed in Karachi.
