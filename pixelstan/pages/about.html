About

a collective for Pakistanis

Pixelstan is a digital home for Pakistanis to collectively reimagine and reclaim our cultural narrative. We're building a vibrant archive of our stories, art, traditions, and perspectives, all through the lens of our own experiences. This is a space to challenge stereotypes, celebrate our diversity, and forge a contemporary understanding of what it means to be Pakistani in the digital age, by us, for us.
