About Radio Pakistan

August 14 1947
After nearly 200 years of British colonial rule, two newly sovereign states emerged in South Asia; India and Pakistan (later Bangladesh)

After the largest mass migration in modern history, much was left to rebuild and recover, both physically and emotionally

Due to political and economic instabilities, Pakistan has often struggled to define itself, which opened doors to religious extremism and thus, political and social instability. This disruption has particularly eroded entertainment and the arts, including the music industry

Music is a core part of culture; no amount of political instability or religious extremism will rid the human desire for expression that music enables

I hope you enjoy this little journey back in time. Thanks for being here :D

an ode to those who kept alive the most universal language of all; music
