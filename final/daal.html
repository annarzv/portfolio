baby's yellow daal

Start off by rinsing your daal, always! then ...

1. Add water and lentils to the pot on medium heat

2. Add salt, a pinch of haldi (turmeric), & laal mirch (red pepper flakes). Stir stir stir

3. After a very long time (1hr-ish), add a tomato or two. This will eventually dissolve and become one with the daal. It adds a nice tang and texture to the experience. Stir stir stir

4. You're daal is almost ready! Add your baghaar & cover the pot so that all the flavors can get acquainted with one another.

Baghaar (tempering) is my favorite part about daal. I've created a recipe for my mum's method - feel free to check it out :-)

Enjoy with rice, roti / naan, or as is!
